About Us

We are a pool of innovators who are fully committed in bringing revolutionary changes in this modern era.The club is furnished with an incubation center,where thoughts can be transformed into reality with the support of our wise and expert advisors. We are intended to shake down the latest technologies and shape it up into a product which can globally be used and accessed to provide comfort and ease in one's life.

Ultimately, our club will guide you on the idea’s technical and business feasibility, practical implementation and profit aspects from the market point of view.

Our Mission

Innovation Club is envisaged to network people, ideas, experiences & resources to galvanize the innovation community in LNMIIT. This club not only helps to nurture your ideas and encourage you to bring up business proposals but there is also an Incubation centre where in after rigorous evaluation of your idea the institute may provide you with the required facilities and perhaps you can even get funds for your project.
